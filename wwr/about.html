Rapid Adventures

At Rapid Adventures, we believe that adventure is essential to the human spirit. Our mission is to connect people with nature through exciting and safe experiences on the most stunning rivers in the region. We're committed to providing not just rafting trips, but moments that become lifelong memories.

History

Founded in 2008 by Rodrigo Reyes, a passionate rafting guide, Rapid Adventures began with just two rafts and a team of three. What started as a small operation on the Colorado River has grown into the region's leading rafting company. Over the past 18 years, we've safely guided more than 3,000 adventurers through challenging and beautiful river routes, always maintaining our commitment to safety and environmental sustainability.
